What is ScamShield App about?
ScamShield has a variety of products and tools that help defend against scams. These products and tools consist of the ScamShield Helpline (1799),
ScamShield app for iPhone and Android, a website (scamshield.gov.sg) containing a range of anti-scam resources, and ScamShield Alert social channels on WhatsApp.

Key Features of the ScamShield App:

  • Blocks scam calls automatically
  • Filters and detects scam SMS messages
  • Checks suspicious links from WhatsApp, Telegram, and SMS
  • Offers 24/7 anti-scam support via the 1799 helpline
  • Provides up-to-date scam alerts and safety tips

Steps to Use the ScamShield App:

  1. Download the ScamShield app from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Install and open the app on your device.
  3. Allow the necessary permissions for call and SMS filtering.
  4. Use the app to check suspicious links and numbers.
  5. Report scam messages or calls directly through the app.
