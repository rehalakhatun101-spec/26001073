What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity
The practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks.
Phishing
A trick where attackers try to steal your personal info by pretending to be someone you trust.
Malware
Bad software designed to harm your computer or steal information.
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
Extra security step that asks for more than just your password to verify your identity.

Top 5 Tips to Stay Safe Online

Follow these simple steps to keep your online accounts and personal information secure:

  1. Create strong, unique passwords for each account.
  2. Turn on multi-factor authentication wherever possible.
  3. Be cautious when clicking links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  4. Keep your software and devices up to date with the latest security patches.
  5. Only download apps and software from trusted sources like official app stores.
