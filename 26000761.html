 5 Tips to Protect Yourself from Online Scams

5 TIPS to Protect Yourself from Online Scams before you close this website ;)

These are more or less the effective methods recommended by people with experience and after some research

#1

Watch Out for Pressure Tactics

Scammers may try to rush or scare you into doing what they want. Take your time and don’t be pushed into decisions.

#2

Verify Before Taking Action

Go straight to a company’s official website to verify contact details before responding to suspicious messages.

#3

Don’t Click on Suspicious Links

Avoid clicking links in emails or messages from unknown or unusual sources—even if they look legitimate.

#4

Use Trusted Payment Methods

Only pay through secure and official channels. Avoid third-party websites or unfamiliar apps for financial transactions.

#5

Report Scams

Whether you were affected or not, reporting helps stop scammers and protects others from becoming victims.
